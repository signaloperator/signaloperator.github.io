Signal Operator

Easy tool to test/debug/drive I2C and SPI - with Arduino, and open source. Take a look at evaluation text to know how it works:

  • soeval.txt (中文/English)

Check out photos of hardware setup, and video of screen recording:

  • https://github.com/signaloperator/soeval/releases

That's it. You can download the code here:

  • https://github.com/signaloperator/sofirmware/releases

Of course it's cross-platform and works on Linux, thanks to Arduino ecosystem:

  • tutorial-1-i2c-at24c01.png
  • tutorial-2-i2c-lm75a.png
  • tutorial-3-spi-w25q128.png
